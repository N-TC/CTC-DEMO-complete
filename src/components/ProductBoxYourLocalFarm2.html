Topline Farms

Out in the fields of Leamington, Ont., Topline’s focus is on the vine. From plump, red tomatoes as big as your palm – the kind you slice, fan out on a plate and drizzle with olive oil, salt and torn basil – to sweet medley cherry tomatoes you can eat by the handful just as they are – and everything in between. Topline also grows cucumbers, and plenty of peppers – minis, sweet bell, spicy and more.
